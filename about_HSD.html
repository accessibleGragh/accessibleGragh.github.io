This tool allows you to explore a heatmap using both visual and audio feedback. A default heatmap is shown when the page loads. Use the W, A, S, D keys or arrow keys to move the black square cursor across the heatmap. As the cursor moves, the system generates different tones based on the color under the cursor. Press P to toggle the image visibility (useful if visual noise is unwanted), and press M to toggle sound on or off.
